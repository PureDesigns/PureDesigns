Welcome to Pure Designs!

My name is Rachel, and I created this website to express the gifts God has given me so that others might be inspired. Another reason for this platform is to share the Gospel of Jesus along with things He has been teaching me in His Word.



Things I have to share:


  • Blog: I keep a blog to share what the Lord is teaching me.

  • Art: This mostly includes my drawings and paintings, but I will also share my wood work and any other things I might enjoy creating.

  • Digital Art: Since my time at Lone Star College, I have enjoyed every once and a while making something through Adobe products to keep my computer artistic skills functional.

  • Websites: Stuff like this website that I made from scratch either during college or just for fun.

  • Balloons: In my current position, I have become a balloon constructor where I build balloon arches, backdrops, or table decorations for celebrations.
