It's More Than A Necklace

By Rachel Wood

July 9, 2023



God wants our hearts not traditions. God didn’t make robots, we must freely give Him our all, our very self.


With relinquishing “self” comes responsibility to focus our eyes on Jesus as our all. No more worrying about me, myself, and I.


It’s a constant discipline to place our sights on the One who first wore the cross. Pick up your cross and walk.


“Then He said to them all, “If anyone desires to come after Me, let him deny himself, and take up his cross daily, and follow Me.” Luke 9:23
